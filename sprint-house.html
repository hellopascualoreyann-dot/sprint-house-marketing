How We Work

Our process,
step by step.

Step 01
Discovery Call

We get to know your brand, goals, and current landscape to determine the best path forward together.

Step 02
Contract Signing & Onboarding

Formalize the engagement with a contract, 50% upfront payment, and an onboarding session to align on all brand details.

Step 03
Market & Content Research

Deep dive into your market, competitors, and audience to inform every creative and strategic decision made.

Step 04
Pre-Auditing & Analysis

A full audit of your existing accounts — identifying what's working, what's not, and where the real growth opportunities are.

Step 05
Set-up & Optimization

Profile clean-up, bio optimization, link-in-bio setup, and platform-specific settings for maximum discoverability.

Step 06
SEO

Social SEO implementation — keyword research, hashtag strategy, and caption optimization for sustained organic reach.

Step 07
Content Planning & Strategy

Strategy prep, content calendar creation, creative direction documentation, and full execution alignment before production.

Step 08
Content Production

For production-package clients: on-location photoshoots and filming. For others, this moves directly to editing, graphic design, and caption writing.

Step 09
Scheduling of Posts

Content queued and scheduled for optimal timing — platform-native, audience-aware, and approved before going live.

Step 10
Monitoring & Analysis

Weekly and monthly performance tracking. Data translated into clear insights and reports delivered directly to you.

Step 11
Engagement & Community

Audience engagement, comment management, and inbox management where applicable — keeping your community warm and responsive.
